PRIVACY POLICY

By Sanatan Mishra

    Advertisements

    The app itself does not display advertisements. However the videos from our embedded YouTube Music player might.

  2. Data Collection

    The Cheer Up! app does not collect any user data. All calculations and analyses are done locally on the device running the application. This app does not have a backend. So, no user data is relayed back to the backend.

    User faces are accessed through the camera in real time only while the app is being used, and this data is not stored anywhere.

    The app contains an embedded YouTube Music player, and any data that may be collected is being collected by YouTube. For more information, go to YouTube's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=en.

  3. Social

    There is no social aspect to this application and no user data is shared with any other users.

  4. APIs and SDKs

    The APIs and SDKs used in this application do not collect any user data and do not have any age screen.

  5. Content

    All content is appropriate for ages 12 and up.
